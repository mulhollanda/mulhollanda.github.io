How can I help?

With 15 years' experience as a cycling planner I can provide:

  • Practical and focussed strategy advice;
  • Effective stakeholder support; and
  • Innovative network plans and designs.

About Me

My favourite part of the day is cycling home from nursery with my daughters Nuala and Niamh. I want to see them travel independently from a young age and grow into healthy, active adults.

I apply the same vision to my work to deliver quality built environments where people come first.

Projects

My career highlights include:

  • 16 years with Cycling Scotland and as a consultant
  • Kick-started the national CAPS in 2008/09
  • Provided the advice that led to the delivery of Glasgow Bike Share
  • Delivered the feasibility and preliminary design of sections of the Oban to Fort William NCN78
  • Managing and co-wrote Cycling by Design (2010)
  • Delivered the options development and appraisal of the Union Canal – Meadows cycle route, Edinburgh
  • Designed and delivered Edinburgh's first protected cycleway, the Meadows – Innocent Route
  • Designed one of Glasgow's first protected cycleways, the Gallowgate - Velodrome Route
  • Delivered a stakeholder engagement process for Edinburgh, described by Sustrans as 'best practice'
  • Developed a stakeholder engagment and promotional programme to tackle the Bears Way 'bikelash'
  • Implemented a project management and staff training system for a community development project in Ghana, and launched school partnerships between Scotland and Ghana www.lucyandy.blogspot.com
